Our Founder

Kaviya

Kaviya College of Engineering was established in 2005 with a vision to provide high-quality education in the field of engineering and technology. Our campus is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and a dedicated team of faculty members who are committed to nurturing the next generation of engineers and innovators.

Our college offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to cater to the evolving needs of the industry. We emphasize practical learning, research, and development to ensure that our students are well-prepared for their careers. Our curriculum is regularly updated to include the latest advancements in technology and engineering.

At Kaviya College of Engineering, we believe in creating a holistic learning environment. Our campus features modern laboratories, a well-stocked library, and various extracurricular activities to foster personal growth and leadership skills. We are dedicated to providing an enriching educational experience that prepares our students for success in a globalized world.
